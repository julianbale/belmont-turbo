Welcome to my Web Site. I have put together a few pictures and some information about my current car which for the last 4 years has been a very rewarding project. The goal of the project was to make a car that was different, fast, safe and fun, not to mention loud.

I have seen another modified Belmont at one or two of the shows but as far as I know I have the only Turbo Belmont MkII in the whole world.
